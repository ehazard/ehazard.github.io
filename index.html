Hello and welcome!

My name is Eric Hazard and I'm a Computer Security student at Norwich University.

Currently I intern at the MITRE Corporation in which I do various work. I am also a full time student at Norwich University in which I'm pursuing a BS in Computer Security and Information Assurance with a concentration in Forensics. At Norwich University, I'm an active member in ACM, NU Soccer Club and I'm an administrator at the Norwich University Center for Advanced Computing and Digital Forensics (NUCAC-DF).

There are three things I'm very passionate about: Technology, Soccer, and Music. I believe these three things are a good barometer for who I am as a person. I really love to embrace technology and see where it will take our society forward. I enjoy attending various start-up events in the Boston area to take note of what areas people are bringing our technology into.
